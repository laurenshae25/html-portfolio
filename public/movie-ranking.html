My Favorite Movies Of All Time!

Top 3 movies.

Apocalypto

This action packed movie kept me on my toes the entire time. I am naturally drawn to the rainforest so I loved the overall setting. The movie was very graphic which helped make the story more realistic, causing it to affect my emotions.

Spirited Away

I loved the animation style of this movie, it was very pleasing to the eye. The writer took you on a journey and I liked the little life lessons that were implemented.

Avatar

Avatar also took place in a rainforest setting which I loved. I like that it was a mix of animation and live action. The magical dreamland was very unique.
